[color=green][head=3]RESURRECTION AND RECOVERY NOTICE[/head][/color]
[bold]OFFICIAL GUILD PUBLIC SERVICE ANNOUNCEMENT[/bold]
◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆
➤[bold]Purpose:[/bold]  
[bullet] To establish clear procedures for the recovery and revival of fallen guild members.
[bullet] To ensure fair compensation for those who aid in recovery, and equitable cost distribution for resurrection rites.
─────────────────────────────────────────  
➤[bold]Procedure:[/bold]  
[bullet] The guild shall arrange resurrection for verified members whose remains are returned intact.  
[bullet] The Guild will cover [color=chocolate]50%[/color] of the resurrection cost; the revived adventurer must reimburse the remaining [color=chocolate]50%[/color].  
[bullet] The individual responsible for retrieving and returning the fallen shall be entitled to compensation of up to [color=silver]2 silver[/color], paid directly by the revived adventurer as gratitude for their effort.  
─────────────────────────────────────────  
➤[bold]Notes:[/bold]  
[bullet] Rewards shall not be granted for recoveries showing evidence of foul play.  
[bullet] Any attempt to falsify deaths, stage incidents, or exploit resurrection benefits will be reported to the guards.  
[bullet] The Guild reserves the right to deny resurrection in cases of misconduct, repeat negligence, or unpaid dues.
─────────────────────────────────────────
[italic]“Life may be restored — but at a price, as all things are.”[/italic]
