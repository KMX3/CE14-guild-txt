[bold]Wind of Sylphoria, Adventurers' Guild[/bold]
[color=orange][head=3]QUARTZ GEODE PROSPECTING[/head][/color]
Quest provider: Wind of Sylphoria
◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆
➤[bold]Requirements:[/bold]
[bullet]Prospect and write down positions of quartz geodes.
[bullet]Must have it confirmed by either an alchemist or a guild worker.
─────────────────────────────────────────
➤[bold]Informations:[/bold]
[bullet]Quartz geodes look like hollow shells inside stone, filled with colored quartz and flying balls of light. Such light means high concentration of magic currents. Such quartz could be used in alchemy. 
[bullet]Balls of light are called essence and could be gathered with special alchemical device (could be given by alchemist).
─────────────────────────────────────────
➤[bold]Rewards:[/bold] 
[bullet]White and orange geodes - 10 silver coins.
[bullet]Yellow, blue and black - 5 silver coins.
[bullet]All other - 2 silver coins.
