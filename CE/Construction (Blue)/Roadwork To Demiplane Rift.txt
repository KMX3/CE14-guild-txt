[bold]Wind of Sylphoria, Adventurers' Guild[/bold]
[color=blue][head=3]ROADWORK TO DEMIPLANE RIFT[/head][/color]
Quest provider: Wind of Sylphoria
◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆
➤[bold]Requirements:[/bold]
[bullet]Build a road from the town's gates to demiplane rift.
─────────────────────────────────────────
➤[bold]Informations:[/bold] 
[bullet]For benefit of arriving adventurers it is beneficial to have a road between town's gate and demiplane entrance.
[bullet]Quest will be completed if you build at least dirt pathway, illuminated with torches.
[bullet]Paving and decorating could be additionally rewarded.
You are provided with instruments and materials by guild (if guild couldn't provide materials, you will be compensated).
─────────────────────────────────────────
➤[bold]Rewards:[/bold] 
[bullet] 1 gold coin.
◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆──◆
[bold]To accept this quest, tear this notice from the guild board and bring it to the counter. Present this slip to a guild worker for signature before departure. Upon completion, return the signed slip to have it stamped [Approved] and receive your reward. Your work will be inspected by a guild clerk upon completion.[/bold]
